Kourtney Kardashian and Travis Barkers 3 Weddings Are Heading to Hulu

Posted by Billy Koelling on Friday, August 30, 2024

Kourtney Kardashian and Travis Barker's three wedding ceremonies will soon be available to watch on Hulu. 'Til Death Do Us Part Kourtney & Travis will premiere on the streaming platform on April 13.

"Kourtney, Travis, and their guests enjoy a luxurious wedding weekend in Portofino, Italy. Private and personal footage reveal an intimate family event full of beautiful moments," the special's synopsis reads.

In a trailer that dropped yesterday, fans can catch glimpses of romantic, never-before-seen footage captured during the couple's infamous three weddings.

"Can you believe we got married three times?" Kardashian asks Barker while the two sit in bed together.

"It's like choosing a child," the Blink-182 drummer responds. "I can't pick the best one."

Kardashian and Barker first wed in a surprise Las Vegas chapel ceremony last April, though it wasn't legal. They officially married in an intimate courthouse ceremony in Santa Barbara in May. Finally, they celebrated their union one last time with a lavish wedding thrown in Portofino, Italy.

"Vegas was like our wild, rock star wedding," Barker reflects. "Santa Barbara was like as traditional as everybody else's wedding could have been."

"And then, Italy was just really romantic and classic," the Poosh founder adds. "I felt like I had so much anxiety because it's such a vulnerable moment you're sharing."

Barker tells his wife, "I just felt like I'm about to marry the love of my life, and then it was like, 'Oh, my God. I'm about to marry the love of my life.' And then, it all set in. … I was nervous, and I knew I'm not going to be able to say my vows without crying."

Their Italian ceremony took place at Domenico Dolce and Stefano Gabbana's Portofino villa. Of crafting the look of the wedding, Kardashian says, "We provided the feeling and the vibe, and then really trusted Domenico to carry it out. We wanted it to feel authentic."

Kardashian's reality TV family, including Kim Kardashian, Kris Jenner, and Kendall Jenner, also make cameos in the trailer.

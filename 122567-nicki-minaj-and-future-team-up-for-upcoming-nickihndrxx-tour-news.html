Nicki Minaj &amp; Future Team Up For Upcoming &quot;NICKIHNDRXX TOUR&quot;

Posted by Tandra Barner on Thursday, August 15, 2024

Nicki Minaj is having a big year. After going on a lengthy social media hiatus for the first few months of 2018, the star is taking over in a major way. Her Queen album may have been delayed for a release in August but that could have something to do with the major announcement she made today. Nicki unexpectedly made some big waves this morning when she announced a joint tour with Future and it's only right for the two to be teaming up for a show of this caliber.

Announcing the NICKIHNDRXX TOUR, Minaj and Future will be headed to a city near you in the next few months. Beginning in September and running through the end of November, Nicki and Fewtch are hitting the road across North America before heading to Europe in 2019.

Doja Cat Reacts To Plastic Surgery Haters In Her Usual Fashion

Posted by Tandra Barner on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

By now, those who still follow Doja Cat on Twitter have become accustomed to the singer's unusual online antics. She's never shied away from sending out random, and sometimes unintelligible messages. More recently, she's been sending out major announcements in her own special way. Already this month she's had some pretty exciting news unveiled online, including a revelation of her breast augmentation, and the name of her upcoming fourth studio album.

When sharing the former with her fans, Doja tweeted, "Got my t*tties done and my cl*t bedazzled." In another post, the Californian confirmed that she underwent another procedure at the same time. "Four days into recovery rn, feels ok. I got lipo so my thighs hurt a lot if I move too much. But I'm healing really fast," she wrote, prompting more questions about the difference between BBLs and liposuction, among other topics, to come pouring in from followers.

Doja Cat's Big Announcement

Aside from the curious inquiries, Doja is also facing some backlash for publicly speaking about her procedures, which could potentially influence others to follow in her footsteps. "Stop encouraging your young and impressionable fans to change their bodies," one person wrote to the Planet Her hitmaker on Sunday (March 26). In response to this, she clapped back, "Eat my long quiet and warm farts." Afterward, her timeline lit up with a "Haha c:," though it remains unclear exactly what the recording artist was giggling at.

Elsewhere on her page, Doja Cat has been boasting about the new and improved appearance of her chest, tweeting, "Wish I could suck my own t*tties. That's how good they look rn, God damn." A curious follower wrote back, "How much longer do you have left to heal? Like, how long does that take?" To this, the 27-year-old confirmed she'll likely be in recovery for a few more weeks, making it three months total since she initially went under the knife to make some changes to her appearance.

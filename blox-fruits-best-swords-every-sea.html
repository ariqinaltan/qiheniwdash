The Best Swords From Every Sea In Blox Fruits

Posted by Kelle Repass on Friday, August 9, 2024

First Published November 4, 2023, 10:19

So, you want the best sword in Blox Fruits? Well, the answer to that will be different for every Sea! And that's precisely why we have made such an in-depth list for you! With this guide, you'll know which are the top weapons to have at your disposal.

We will go over the best swords from every Sea in Blox Fruits! Leaving no stone unturned so your arsenal is stacked with only the greatest weapons. So get ready for battle, as getting all these blades may be harder than you think!

Note from Editor: This guide is accurate up to Blox Fruits version 21 ("Ghost update").

Table of Contents

What Are The Best First Sea Swords In Blox Fruits?

Dark Blade

The best sword in the First Sea in Blox Fruits is the Dark Blade! This should be no surprise, as the blade deals high damage, has solid combo potential, and has very low mastery requirements. The best part? We have a detailed guide on how to get Dark Blade in Blox Fruits.

The Saber

In the second place, we have The Saber! This sword may seem very basic initially, but don't underestimate it—it is super strong. It has both Deadly Rush and Triple Slash, the latter of which is a massive projectile dealing huge damage. It's hard to miss the attack!

What Are The Best Second Sea Swords In Blox Fruits?

Rengoku 

The best sword of the Second Sea in Blox Fruits is the Rengoku—yep, the same one from the anime and manga Demon Slayer. This crazy demon-slaying blade is not one to disappoint! The best ability it has is the Burning Slash! So, if you feel like letting off giant fire attacks and killing most people in one hit, this is the sword for you!

Gravity Cane

The second-best sword in the Second Sea in Blox Fruits is the Gravity Cane! That's right, this once-forgotten sword has benefitted greatly from all the Blox Fruits updates, and now it is the second most powerful sword in the Second Sea. With abilities like Dimensional Grasp and Lunar Blitz, no one will stand in your way!

What Are The Best Third Sea Swords In Blox Fruits?

Shark Anchor

The best sword in the Third Sea in Blox Fruits is none other than The Shark Anchor! I don't think many would see this as a surprise right now, given just how powerful it is. If you truly want the Shark Anchor, check out our guide on how to get it above! 

Cursed Dual Katana

The second-best sword in the Third Sea in Blox Fruits is the Cursed Dual Katana! Like the Shark Anchor, this is one of the hardest, if not the hardest, swords to get in Blox Fruits. With abilities like Revolving Ravager and Slayer of Goliath, you are in danger from no one!

And that concludes our guide to the best swords from every Sea in Blox Fruits! We hope you enjoyed it. Now that you're finished reading this guide, you may want to check out our Tiki Outpost guide while you are at it! Or perhaps you are looking for a Cold Heart?

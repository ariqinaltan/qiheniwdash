Career

Sharon Osbourne after becoming her husband’s manager gained much experience and then opened her own management company named Sharon Osbourne Management. This company has managed star artists from groups like Coal Chamber, Lita Ford, The Smashing Pumpkins, and more.

Following her career as a management firm owner, Sharon gained more popularity when she appeared in the MTV reality show “The Osbournes”. This show was scripted to show the real life of Sharon’s husband and kids. This show telecasted from 2002 to 2005 helped Sharon in becoming an international sensation.

In 2003 she worked as the host of the “The Sharon Osbourne Show” constructed by her. Her fame helped her to be a judge on UK’s The X-Factor from 2004 to 2007.

After 2007 she remained a part of the American Got Talent Judge panel. She performed as a judge for many years and worked with personalities like Piers Morgan, David Hasselhoff, Brandy Norwood, Howie Mandel, and Howard Stern.

She performed as host of “The X-Factor” from 2004–2007, 2013, and 2016–2017. Sharon was last seen on American Panel Talk show on Fox News channel titled The Five as a Guest Co-host.

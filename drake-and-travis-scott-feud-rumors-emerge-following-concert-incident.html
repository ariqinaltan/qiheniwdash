Drake and Travis Scott Feud Rumors Emerge Following Concert Incident

Posted by Tandra Barner on Thursday, August 15, 2024

Rumors are swirling about a potential feud between two titans of the industry: Drake and Travis Scott. The speculation has ignited after an eyebrow-raising incident during a recent performance, leaving fans on the edge of their seats and wondering about the state of their relationship.

During a live show, Drake appeared to throw subtle shots at Travis Scott while performing their collaboration track “Meltdown.” The cryptic lyrics and gestures from Drake have raised questions about the underlying tensions between the two artists.

Adding fuel to the fire, Travis Scott‘s recent actions have been under scrutiny. He was spotted supporting Future and Metro Boomin even before they released their Kendrick Lamar-assisted diss track aimed at Drake. At the Rolling Loud festival, Travis was reportedly seen encouraging the duo to play the controversial song for the unsuspecting audience, further intensifying speculations of a rift between him and Drake.

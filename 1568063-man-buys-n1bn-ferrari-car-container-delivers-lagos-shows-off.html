Man &quot;Buys&quot; 2023 Ferrari Car Worth N1b, Says Customs Got Almost N400m for Clearing, Nigerians React

Posted by Kelle Repass on Wednesday, August 28, 2024
  • A man who shipped a 2023 SF90 Ferrari car to Lagos said that customs got almost N400m to clear it at the port
  • The young man placed the value of the car at N1b as he took delivery of the car and test-drove it around
  • A young Nigerian man who got his RAV 4 said that customs duty on his vehicle was not as much as it is now

A Nigerian man who deals in expensive fast cars has made a video of a new SF90 Ferrari vehicle he shipped to Lagos state.

The car's delivery was made with a very big shipping container, and the moments of delivery were well captured.

N1bn Ferrari car

He (@the_razz_guy) stated that the he got car's price was N1b. Many people were amazed at how expensive the Ferrari was.

In one of his responses to people questioning the price, he said he paid customs around N400m to clear it at the port.

According to CarandDriver, the car's starting price is $528,765 (N637,940,167.08).

Speaking with Aanu, who bought a Toyota RAV 4 in 2019, he said though custom duty on his vehicle was not high, it seemed to have increased in recent years.

"What scares people from getting cars from auction sites from abroad is what they may end up paying much to clear it at the port. My RAV 4 customs duty was not much. Perhaps I was lucky. What people pay now is now very high."

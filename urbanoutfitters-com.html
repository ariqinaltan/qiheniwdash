Urban Outfitters promo codes - 10% Off in July 2024

Posted by Billy Koelling on Sunday, August 4, 2024

FAQs

How can I get free shipping at Urban Outfitters?

Get free shipping on purchases at Urban Outfitters when you spend $50 or over. If your order is less than $50, standard shipping costs $6.95. 

When is the Urban Outfitters sale?

Shop the Urban Outfitters sales at key times of the year, such as Christmas or Black Friday. Last year, UO gave 25% off purchases for Black Friday sales. If you can’t wait for a seasonal sale, simply go to the dedicated ‘sale’ page on the UO website. 

Does Urban Outfitters have newsletter discount?

Yes, sign up for the Urban Outfitters newsletter to get 15% off your first purchase. You’ll also be kept updated on the latest flash sales, exclusive Urban Outfitters promo codes, and new clothing.  

Does Urban Outfitters have birthday coupons?

Yes. There has previously been an Urban Outfitters coupon code for 15% off to customers during their birthday month. Previous promotions required shoppers to sign up for the UO Rewards program and download the app to receive this promo code for Urban Outfitters.

Can I earn Urban Outfitters rewards?

Yes. Sign up for the Urban Outfitters rewards program—UO Rewards—to be sent a 10% off Urban Outfitters coupon on your first purchase. To sign up, visit the Urban Outfitters website and enter your preferred email address in the sign-up box. You’ll then be able to earn points on your orders, which will convert to a $5 coupon for every 100 points.  

What is the Urban Outfitters returns policy?

Urban Outfitters accepts returns for clothing and furniture within 30 days. If you return your order after this, then your refund will be a store credit. Your order should be unworn or unused. There are some products that cannot be returned, including final sale items. Returns include a $5 restocking fee.  

Hints and Tips

Newsletter savings: Sign up for the Urban Outfitters newsletter and you’ll be sent 15% off your first order. You’ll also be updated on last-minute sales, exclusive discounts, and new products. You can also sign up for UO Rewards with your email to earn points on your orders. When you reach 100 points, you’ll get a $5 reward coupon. 

Seasonal sales: Shop the seasonal and Black Friday sales at Urban Outfitters for some of the brand’s best-known savings. In previous years, the brand has had 25% off sitewide on its website for Black Friday. If you missed Black Friday sales, Urban Outfitters has a dedicated ‘sale’ page on its website throughout the year. This is a perfect opportunity for shopping trending clothing or home items for less. 

Shop Clearance: Explore the dedicated ‘sale’ clearance page on the Urban Outfitters website for savings all year round. You can shop across multiple departments, from women’s apparel to furniture. We’ve previously noted up to 40% off selected items during limited-time sales. Check our sales updates and discount codes for the latest sales news.

The UO App: Download the Urban Outfitters app to get app-exclusive discounts and promotion updates. You can download the app for free. You’ll also be able to create wish lists, earn and track rewards, and possibly get an Urban Outfitters birthday discount.

How do you melee fight in Red Dead Redemption 2?

Answer

There are two buttons to memorise whenever a fistfight starts in Red Dead Redemption 2: Square and Circle on the PS4 and X and B on the Xbox One. The first of these buttons allows you to defend yourself against strikes, while the second allows you to punch your opponent with a fist.

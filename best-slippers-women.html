15 Best Slippers for Women

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, August 6, 2024

UGG's best-selling Ansley slipper is a favorite for good reason. The classic, slip-on loafer shape allows for ease while pulling on and off. Made with the brand's signature wool lining, the fluffy inside will keep you snuggly on the coldest of days, without causing excess sweat.

Reviewers agree that the warmth is unparalleled. "These are my new favorite slide ons! They are very soft and comfortable," one says. "They will definitely keep your feet warm! I love how I can wear them with or without socks and still be comfortable."

Sizes: 5-12 US, also available in wide sizes

Colorways: Chestnut, black, light grey, samba red, espresso, lighthouse, purple ruby, wildflower, hickory/sand, pink blossom and slate

Materials: Suede upper, 100% wool lining with rubber sole

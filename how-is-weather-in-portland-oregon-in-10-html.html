How Is The Weather In Portland Oregon In December

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, August 5, 2024
People are currently reading this guide.

So You Want to Visit Portland in December: Prepare for a Chill (Literally) Adventure!

Ah, Portland in December. The twinkling lights twinkle a little brighter, the coffee shops seem extra steamy, and the locals? Well, they're practically hibernating with cozy sweaters and mugs of hot cocoa. But hey, that shouldn't stop you from braving the elements for a unique Pacific Northwest experience!

Don't Expect Sunshine and Rainbows (But Maybe Rainbows... Kinda)

Let's be honest, December in Portland isn't exactly known for its sun-drenched beaches. We're talking cool, crisp air (think average highs in the 40s Fahrenheit, that's around 4-7 Celsius) and a healthy dose of rain. But hey, that's kind of our thing!

Here's the upside: Portland rain is more of a drizzle than a downpour, perfect for curling up with a good book in a cozy coffee shop (because let's face it, that's what you were probably going to do anyway). Plus, the wet streets mean the city gets this cool, misty vibe that's straight out of a detective novel. Think Humphrey Bogart in a trench coat, but with a reusable water bottle (gotta be eco-friendly, you know?).

Pro Tip: Pack a waterproof jacket and some comfy shoes. Puddles may form, but they're more for splashing than submerging your boots in.

Embrace the Holiday Cheer (and Maybe Avoid the Holiday Crowds)

December in Portland is a wonderland of twinkling lights, festive decorations, and enough holiday cheer to fill Santa's sack. Think charming Christmas markets, carolers bursting into song at unexpected corners (in a good way, of course), and that special Portland eggnog that's rumored to have magical properties (it might just be the alcohol, but hey, no judgment).

However, December can also be a busy time, especially around Christmas and New Year's. If you're looking for a more low-key experience, consider visiting in early December. You'll still get all the festive feels without the crowds.

Embrace the Layers, my Friend

Remember that cool, crisp air we mentioned? Yeah, that means packing layers is key. Think thermals, a good sweater, and a waterproof jacket. You might even want to throw in a hat and gloves, just in case. But hey, on the bright side, layering allows for maximum outfit creativity!

Packing Tip: Don't forget comfy walking shoes! Portland is a walkable city, and exploring the festive streets is a great way to soak up the atmosphere.

FAQ: How to Survive (and Thrive) in Portland in December

  • How to pack for Portland in December? Layers are your friend! Pack thermals, sweaters, a waterproof jacket, hat, and gloves.
  • How to avoid the crowds in December? Visit in early December for a less crowded experience.
  • How to get around Portland in December? The city is walkable, but public transportation is also a great option.
  • How to find the best holiday events? Check out local event listings or ask your friendly neighborhood barista (they always have the inside scoop).
  • How to enjoy the rain? Grab a coffee, find a cozy coffee shop with a view, and curl up with a good book. Embrace the hygge!

Kim Kardashian Wears Nude Black Lace Leggings to Balenciaga Show

Posted by Billy Koelling on Saturday, August 17, 2024

Kim Kardashian attended Balenciaga's Fall 2024 Show in a cool high-fashion look that had a sporty edginess, a futuristic touch, and, of course, some skin tight elements.

The fashion house—for which Kardashian has served as an ambassador since 2022—took over Los Angeles’ Windsor Square on Saturday to showcase creative director Demna’s collection of activewear, grunge, and eveningwear designs.

Stefanie Keenan

The Skims founder stepped out in nude pantaleggings layered with black lace by Balenciaga, an athletic zip-up jacket also by the luxury fashion brand, and a nude form-fitting turtleneck. Kardashian styled her dark tresses in a wavy wet hair look and accessorized with oversized black reflective sunglasses. She left her Birkin at home for the outing, instead opting for a black-and-brown reusable nylon bag from Erewhon in promotion of the upscale supermarket’s collaboration with Balenciaga. A bouquet of flowers poked out the top of the bag, which Kardashian told Variety she was going to give to Denma.

Stefanie Keenan

The American Horror Story actor posed for photos with her younger sister Kendall Jenner, who showed her support for Balenciaga in a black figure-hugging gown by the brand. Jenner’s elegant dress featured a trailing cape and a scoop neckline. She let the piece make a statement, keeping her jewelry minimal with chunky gold hoops and pulling her brunette locks back into a low ponytail.

Kardashian made a grand appearance in brisk New York City on Thursday in cozy layers. Ever the maximalist, she donned a massive gray faux-fur coat over a black zip-up hoodie and matching drawstring sweatpants. To make things even more luxe, she finished off the look with a gray crocodile Hermès Birkin weekender bag and Balenciaga’s sleek knife booties.

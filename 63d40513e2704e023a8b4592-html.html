Jessica Simpson net worth: Her fortune and how much is her business worth?

Posted by Patria Henriques on Saturday, August 24, 2024

Jessica Simpson is an American actress, singer, entrepreneur and TV celebrity that was born on July 10, 1980 in Abilene, Texas, growing up in Waco and Dallas, Texas.

Simpson has been a known star since the 2000's as she raised to stardom in the same era as other pop superstars like Britney Spears or Christina Aguilera.

She had a succesful career in music, but majorly focused on acting and becoming a reality TV star, with her series alongside her then husband, Nick Lachey, "Newlyweds: Nick and Jessica" being a huge breakthroughfor her career in 2002.

Her most famous movie made was "The Dukes of Hazzard" alongside Johnny Knoxville and Sean William Scott, which was a huge box office success around the world.

Jessica Simpson net worth

Jessica Simpson has found huge success as an entrepreneur, with her retail products like handbags, shoes, lingerie and perfume having sold many billions since their launching, which in consequence has made her have an estimated net worth of $200 million dollars, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

At the peak of the brand's value in 2014, it had sales for over $1 billion dollars, with the 2021 sales still being for over $500 million.

Despite popular belief, Simpson is not a billionaire, despite her business ventures having generated billions of dollars in sales.

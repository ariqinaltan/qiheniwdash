Tiffany Haddish Spoke With Beyonce &amp; She's Cool About The Biting Story

Posted by Billy Koelling on Thursday, August 8, 2024

When Tiffany Haddish revealed that a woman "on drugs" bit Beyonce in the face at an exclusive Hollywood party, the internet went into a frenzy. People started coming out with lists of their own, alleging it was Sanaa Lathan, Lena Dunham, Sara Foster or Taraji P. Henson. Sanna definitely took the most heat and she responded to the blame saying it's just "bizarre."

“I adore Beyoncé,” she said. “I would never do anything malicious like that — to her, or to anyone. It’s so bizarre. I thought it was the funniest thing ever that it was a news story. It’s so crazy.”

Since the incident (and before Tiffany could tell us who it was) the actress was forced to sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement by Beyonce. You would think Bey would have been a little upset that her business was being spread to the media, but according to Tiffany, she's cool with it. “I didn’t think it was going to blow up like that,” Tiffany told PEOPLE recently. “We’ve talked since then, and it’s been cool, Nobody’s trippin’ except for everybody else.”

Tiffany has had small acting roles throughout her career, but she really blew up on the scene in last year's Girls Trip. “My life has changed in so many ways, but not that many ways,” she said of her new found fame. “The only thing is I work on a regular basis and I’m able to provide for my family even better now.”

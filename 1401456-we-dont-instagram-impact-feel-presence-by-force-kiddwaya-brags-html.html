Posted by Kelle Repass on Tuesday, August 13, 2024

- Big Brother Naija ex-housemate, Kiddwaya, took to social media to brag after it appeared that his Instagram account became invisible for a while

- Kidd noted that becoming invisible or leaving an app does not stop the world from feeling his greatness

- Hours later, the reality star announced that his page was back up and active

While many celebrities would be devastated after losing their Instagram accounts or even having it inactive for days, BBNaija's Kiddwaya has stated that it is not a big deal.

He said:

"Forget!!! We don’t need Instagram to make an impact. They will feel our presence by force. We too blown."

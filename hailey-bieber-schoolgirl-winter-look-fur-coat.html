Hailey Bieber's Preppy Schoolgirl Look Includes a Feather Coat

Posted by Patria Henriques on Thursday, August 22, 2024

No one ever said you can’t still show a little skin once winter rolls around, and Hailey Bieber proved that true with her latest outfit.

The 27-year-old braved the cold in an ensemble from the N21 Spring/Summer 2024 collection. Styled by Dani Michelle, the outfit included a baby-pink cami layered over a lacy white bra and a shimmering low-rise sequin miniskirt in gold. Bieber accessorized the schoolgirl look with her giant diamond B (for Bieber) necklace.

While these teeny-tiny pieces may not be ideal for a snowy day, fret not—the Rhode founder added a cream feather coat from Robert Cavalli that helped warm things up. The shaggy vintage piece was pulled from the brand’s Fall/Winter 2005 collection.

She showed off the ensemble in an Instagram slideshow, which she captioned, “Sugarplum.” But if we’re being honest, the outfit gives us less sugarplum and more early Britney Spears.

via Numero Ventuno on Instagram//Instagram

The oversize winter coat is just the latest piece of statement outerwear Bieber has worn this week.

Just a few days ago, the model enjoyed a weekend getaway in Aspen with her husband, Justin Bieber, and her good friend Kendall Jenner. On one of the group’s nights out, Bieber was spotted in a long gray shearling coat from Ferragamo. She contrasted the neutral outerwear with stark black leather gloves and stiletto pointed-toe boots from Jimmy Choo. She added some fuzz with a massive furry black clutch bag.

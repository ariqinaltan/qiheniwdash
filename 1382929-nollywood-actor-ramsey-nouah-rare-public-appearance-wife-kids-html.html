Nollywood actor and filmmaker, Ramsey Nouah, might be a public figure but the man surely knows how to keep his private life away from prying eyes.

However, the much-loved movie star who rarely shows off his family members made an appearance with them at a recent movie premiere in Lagos.

Nouah was seen on the red carpet at the premiere of his latest movie, Rattlesnake, alongside his wife and children.

In the heartwarming video, the proud dad had his arms around his wife and children as they all posed for photographers on the red carpet.

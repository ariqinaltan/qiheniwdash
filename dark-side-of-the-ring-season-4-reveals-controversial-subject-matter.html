Dark Side Of The Ring Season 4 Reveals Controversial Subject Matter

Posted by Billy Koelling on Thursday, August 22, 2024

Vince might be filing for bankruptcy, but Dark Side of the Ring season 4 is still coming for fans. Now, we have an even better look at the season, so we know what to expect.

We previously covered some of the subjects that they will cover next season on Dark Side of the Ring. The 4th season could be the show’s best, because that Marty Jannetty episode is supposed to be “insane.”

Season 4 of Dark Side of the Ring is set to include Abdullah the Butcher, Bam Bam Bigelow, Chris Candido & Tammy Sytch, Marty Jannetty, Magnum TA, Adrian Adonis, Junkyard Dog, Doink the Clown, Bash at the Beach 2000, and The Graham Family.

The new trailer for Vice’s Dark Side of the Ring includes a lot of new moments. We also see a few extra faces like Mick Foley, Jim Ross, and Tony Atlas in the new video. It wasn’t specifically stated who anyone was refereeing to in the clips during that trailer, but the mood was certainly set.

Themes of drugs, sex, and racism will be featured in the next season of Dark Side of the Ring. We can only imagine that Junkyard Dog’s episode will be hard to watch for a lot of people, but that probably goes for a lot of the episodes next season. Shawn Micahels, who had an affair with Tammy Lynn Sytch for some time, might also be afraid of what will come out during Tammy and Chris Candido’s episode.

The Marty Jannetty episode of Dark Side of the Ring will be insane. Only time will tell if fans consider that the most outlandish episode when the season airs. You can check out the new trailer for Dark Side of the Ring season 4 below.

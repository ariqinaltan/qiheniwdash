Ivan Bart Wife: Was Ivan Bart Married?

Posted by Patria Henriques on Thursday, August 8, 2024

The demise of Ivan Bart, a titan of the fashion industry who left a magnificent legacy in the modeling and fashion industries, has shocked the world of fashion.

Before taking on the role of company president in 2014, Bart worked as an agent for IMG Model in 1994.

Throughout his lengthy career, Bart guided the paths of some of the most well-known faces in fashion.

Fans are curious about his personal life after his “unexpected” death, like who his devastated spouse is and if he had any children. What we know is as follows.

Ivan Bart, was he married?

Before passing away in October 2023, Ivan Bart had a male partner and was a proud member of the LGBTQ community.

TheSun reports that the gatekeeper of fashion was wed to Grant Greenberg.

It is difficult to determine how the couple met, where their wedding was conducted, and how long they were together because their love story was never posted online.

Grant posted a memorial on Instagram for the late Ivan. A photo purports to show Grant holding Ivan in a restaurant. The pair grinned at the camera.

“Ivan Bart, 1963 – 2023” was the caption Grant added to the photo, along with an emoji of a shattered heart.

Malmaison Newcastle Hotel Review | Travel

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, August 6, 2024
Newcastle upon Tyne, Tyne and Wear, England

In an impressive location overlooking the River Tyne on Newcastle’s buzzy Quayside. In keeping with the Malmaison group, the atmosphere is sultry and romantic; low lighting and dark décor throughout. Find also: a sizeable cocktail bar, decent brasserie, and underground spa.

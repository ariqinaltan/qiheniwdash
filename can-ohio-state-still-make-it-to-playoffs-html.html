Can Ohio State Still Make It To The Playoffs

Posted by Kelle Repass on Wednesday, August 21, 2024
People are currently reading this guide.

The Buckeyes and the Playoff Picture: A Tale of "Maybe" with a Side of "Hail Mary"

Buckeye Nation, buckle up! We're diving headfirst into the murky waters of the College Football Playoff picture, specifically the question that's keeping scarlet and gray hearts racing: Can Ohio State claw its way into the final four?

Let's be honest, things aren't looking peachy keen. The Buckeyes don't control their own destiny (insert dramatic music here). But fear not, fellow fans, because where there's a will, there's a way...and a healthy dose of delusion.

The Path Less Traveled (and Requires a Miracle or Two)

Here's the deal: a series of dominoes need to fall perfectly, with a sprinkle of chaos on the side. We're talking upsets, close calls, and maybe even a rogue squirrel causing a fumble on a crucial play. (Hey, it could happen!)

  • The Big Ten Needs a Cinderella Story: Michigan, the conference's current golden boy, has gotta lose. And not just lose, but get absolutely clobbered. We're talking a David vs Goliath situation, with David sporting a maize and blue helmet and a slingshot full of self-doubt.
  • The Rest of the Country Needs to Play Musical Chairs: Several other one-loss teams are vying for those coveted playoff spots. We need them to start tripping over each other like toddlers in a sugar rush. Texas needs to stumble, Alabama can't win out, and that surprise team from the Pac-12? Let's hope they decide to take a well-deserved nap during championship weekend.

But Wait, There's More! (A Glimmer of Hope?)

Now, before you write off the Buckeyes entirely, there's a sliver of hope. Ohio State put up a valiant fight throughout the season. They've got a strong resume and a passionate fanbase that could rival a cult (don't worry, we're a cool cult...mostly). If the committee is feeling generous, and the stars align just so, maybe, just maybe, they'll squeeze the Buckeyes in.

But let's be real, this scenario requires a miracle on the scale of the parting of the Red Sea. So, while we hold onto a sliver of hope, it's probably wise to start mentally preparing for a New Year's Six bowl game instead of a confetti shower in the national championship.

FAQ: Buckeye Playoff Edition

How to channel your inner cheerleader while acknowledging the reality of the situation?

Easy! Support the Buckeyes no matter what, but also stock up on some antacids. You'll need them for the emotional rollercoaster ride.

How to convince your friends from rival fanbases that Ohio State absolutely deserves a spot in the playoffs?

Just smile politely and remind them that their team choked. They'll probably get defensive and the conversation will move on. Trust me, it's for the best.

How to prepare for the inevitable heartbreak if Ohio State gets snubbed?

Stock up on ice cream and tissues. We've all been there, Buckeye Nation. We'll get through this together.

How to ensure you're the most knowledgeable Ohio State fan at the next tailgate?

Memorize this entire post. Bonus points for dramatic reenactments of the possible upset scenarios.

How to (realistically) enjoy the rest of the college football season?

Appreciate the amazing athletes on the field, root for some upsets that benefit the Buckeyes, and savor the camaraderie of Buckeye Nation. After all, that's what college football is all about, right? (Except for the heartbreak, maybe we can skip that part.)

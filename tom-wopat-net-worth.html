Early Life and Education of Tom Wopat

Tom Wopat was born on September 9, 1951, in Lodi, Wisconsin, to parents Albin Carl and Ruth Arlene Wopat. He grew up along with his six siblings on a dairy farm, where he worked as a teenager. Career of Tom Wopat

Tom Wopat began his career as a stage actor, appearing in several productions such as I Love My Wife, City of Angels, and Annie Get Your Gun. After the success of Dukes of Hazzard, Wopat starred in several television shows, including Smallville, Blue Bloods, and Law & Order: Special Victims Unit.

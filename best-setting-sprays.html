The 15 Best Setting Sprays for Long-Lasting Makeup 2023

Posted by Billy Koelling on Sunday, August 25, 2024

What is setting spray, anyway?

Setting spray is an alcohol- or water-based product that prolongs the wear of your makeup for several hours.

How should you use setting spray?

Spray on two to three spritzes, from about six inches away from your face (any closer and you risk messing up your makeup). Then, be sure to let the spray set for about 10 seconds.
